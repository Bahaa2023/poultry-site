About us

We offer workshops and resources to help both novice and experienced poultry enthusiasts learn and grow in their knowledge of heritage breeds and sustainable farming practices. Whether you're a seasoned farmer or just starting out, there's always something new to discover at our farm. So come visit us and experience the difference that passion, dedication, and heritage make. Whether you're seeking to start your own flock, stock up on farm-fresh eggs, or simply immerse yourself in the beauty of rural life, our farm is here to welcome you with open arms. Join us in celebrating the timeless allure of heritage breed chickens and the enduring legacy of traditional farming practices.

Sussex

Light sussex

Speckeled sussex

Buff sussex

Originating from Sussex, England, the Sussex chicken breed is known for its gentle temperament and excellent foraging abilities. Dating back to the early 20th century, Sussex chickens were initially bred for both meat and eggs. We have them in three varieties: light, speckled, and buff Sussex. Sussex hens are prolific layers, typically producing around 250 to 300 large, brown eggs per year. Their robust nature makes them well-suited for free-range environments, and they adapt well to various climates.

We supply hatching eggs from April to June and sell chicks from April to September. Contact us to check availability. Interesting Fact: Sussex chickens are often chosen for their dual-purpose qualities, as they excel in both egg production and meat quality.

Barred Plymouth Rock

The Barred Plymouth Rock, a heritage breed originating from the United States, holds a special place in the hearts of poultry enthusiasts. Known for its striking barred plumage and robust nature, this breed boasts a rich history dating back to the early 19th century. Barred Plymouth Rocks are prized for their dual-purpose qualities, excelling in both egg production and meat quality. Hens are prolific layers, typically producing around 200 to 280 large, brown eggs per year. Their docile temperament and adaptability to various climates make them a popular choice for backyard flocks and small-scale farming operations.

At our farm, we offer hatching eggs from March to May and sell chicks from April to August. Whether you're starting a new flock or expanding your existing one, our Barred Plymouth Rock chicks are sure to delight with their charm and productivity. Contact us today to check availability and begin your journey with this beloved breed.

Rhode island red

The Rhode Island Red, a quintessential American breed, holds a special place in the hearts of poultry enthusiasts worldwide. Originating from the United States, this iconic breed is celebrated for its hardiness, vibrant plumage, and exceptional egg-laying capabilities. Rhode Island Reds are renowned for their prolific egg production, with hens laying around 200 to 300 large, brown eggs per year.
